About Smart Feed AI

Smart Feed AI is a revolutionary application designed to help farmers optimize their dairy operations. Our platform allows you to meticulously track the health and progress of each cow in your herd. You can record daily feed intake and analyze its impact on milk production, enabling you to make data-driven decisions.

Our mission is to empower farmers with the tools they need to improve efficiency, increase profitability, and ensure the well-being of their livestock. Join us on the journey to a smarter, more sustainable future for dairy farming.
